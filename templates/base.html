ConnectYou Code of Conduct

"Be Kind. Be Honest. Do the Right Thing."

👩‍🔧 For Service Providers
  • Be on Time – Show up when you say you will. Let the customer know if you’re running late.
  • Be Respectful – Talk nicely. No rude words, no flirting, no making people uncomfortable.
  • Do Your Best Work – Fix what needs fixing. Clean up after yourself. Be proud of your work.
  • Tell the Truth – Be honest about what you can and can’t do. No sneaky stuff.
  • Keep It Safe – Use tools and materials the right way. Don’t take risks that could hurt someone.
🏡 For Customers
  • Be Kind – Treat providers like real people — with kindness and respect.
  • Be Clear – Explain what you need help with. Be honest about your budget and timeline.
  • Give Feedback – Leave a review after the job — it helps others!
  • Pay Fairly – Pay what was agreed on. No ghosting, no games.
🚫 What’s Not Okay (For Everyone)
  • No bullying or shouting
  • No stealing or breaking things on purpose
  • No lying or pretending to be someone you’re not
  • No funny business — we’re a safe place for real help
✅ What Happens If Rules Are Broken?

We take action if someone breaks the rules. It could mean a warning, a pause on their account, or being removed from ConnectYou. We want everyone to feel safe, respected, and taken care of — every time. It doesn’t matter if you use ConnectYou or anywhere.

Conflict Resolutions

Accountability is key to building long-term trust on ConnectYou. Here are three best practices we follow if something goes wrong:

✅ 1. Transparent & Fast Issue Resolution System
  • Offer in-app or email-based support with live chat and a clear escalation path.
  • Set expectations, e.g., “We’ll respond within 24 hours, and resolve within 3 business days.”
  • Track all complaints in a ticketing system for auditability and pattern recognition.
✅ 2. Fair & Documented Dispute Mediation Policy
  • Establish a neutral mediation process with documented steps for both parties.
  • Require evidence (photos, receipts, messages) from both sides before making a decision.
  • If ConnectYou makes a final call (e.g., issuing a refund, suspending a provider), explain clearly why it was made.
✅ 3. Provider Quality & Conduct Standards (with Enforcement)
  • Create and publicly share this Code of Conduct for providers and users.
  • Use a rating and review system to detect repeated issues early.
  • Enforce consequences fairly: warnings → temporary suspension → permanent removal.

Thank you for helping us keep ConnectYou safe, respectful, and helpful for everyone! 💙
